Case Study — MLB

Stadium Materials Become Recognition Worth Remembering

The Challenge

MLB's previous recognition was a traditional trophy box; generic, off-the-shelf, and disconnected. They wanted to create something that reflected the league's commitment to sustainability.

The Materials Used

Aluminum can nameplates, polypropylene dots, nacho helmets, and HDPE stadium seats in the colors of each winning team.

The Outcome

Custom sustainability trophies aligned to each team's league brand and values. A custom video documented the process from material recovery to presentation, giving MLB content for social media and communications.
